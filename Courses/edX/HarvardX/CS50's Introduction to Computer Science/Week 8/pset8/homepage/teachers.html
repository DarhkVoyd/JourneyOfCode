David J. Malan

David J. Malan is Gordon McKay Professor of the Practice of Computer Science at Harvard University. He teaches Computer Science 50, otherwise known as CS50, which is among Harvard University’s largest courses, one of Yale University’s largest courses, and edX’s largest MOOC.

Brian Yu
